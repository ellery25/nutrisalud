FAQ

Questions, answered

Is NutriSalud free?

Yes. NutriSalud is free to download and use. The core experience — meal discovery, the food journal, goals and educational content — has no paywall.

Is this medical advice?

No. NutriSalud is educational. It can help you notice patterns and learn about nutrition, but it does not diagnose or treat any condition. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about symptoms or dietary changes.

Where do the recipes come from?

Recipes are sourced from TheMealDB, a free public recipe database, including ingredients, step-by-step instructions and video tutorials where available.

Does it work offline?

Partly. Your saved favorites and your food journal work fully offline. Searching for new recipes requires an internet connection.

Which platforms are supported?

NutriSalud is launching on Android first. An iOS version is planned — follow the project on GitHub for updates.
